Junior Youth Notes

Healing Ministry Church – Waterberg | Age Group: 13 - 15

1

The Holy Bible

  • Written by Men, inspired by God, hence the Word of God
  • God breathed knowledge into their spirit (minds) and they began to write it down.
  • No one should add or remove anything written in this Holy book. Rev 22:18-19
  • The Holy Scriptures are written so that we may believe that Jesus is the Christ. John 20:31
2

Be Principled

  • Don't do or say what you feel or are emotional about...feelings and emotions can lie. Rom 1:21-32
  • You don't become a man because you feel like a man, or animal because you feel animalistic. You are a man because God created you that way.
  • God never created gays/lesbians. He is not the God of confusion. 1Cor 14:33

And that from childhood you have known the Holy Scriptures, which are able to make you wise for salvation through faith which is in Christ Jesus. 2Tim 3:15-16

  • Sexual related activities are not for experiment/excitement... 1Cor 6:9-10
  • Love does not mean friendship. Hatred can corrupt your destiny.
  • Learn the right things and do them. This is the FEAR OF THE LORD. Daniel 3:18, 1Sam 15:23
  • Develop courage to say no to the wrong things. Leviticus 18:6, 22-24
3

Honor your Parents/Leaders

Eph 6:1-3
  • Learn to respect your parents always...even when they are drunk.
  • Learn to respect your leaders/pastors
4

The Power of Consistency

  • Read (bible) and pray every day
  • Study your school books daily
5

Prayer

  • It is a way to communicate with God: Ask, knock, seek... Matt 7:7-8
  • A means to change things and strengthen oneself. Acts 12:5, Jam 5:16
  • FASTING is another means to boost our prayers. Matt 17:21, Esther 4:15-16
6

Jesus is Lord and King Now

  • Jesus is the only Saviour of the world. Acts 4:12
  • The devil is a defeated enemy, bound by Christ through the cross. Col 2:15, Jam 4:7
7

Our Identity

  • You are not what your feelings or people say you are, but what God says you are.
  • You are God's workmanship created for his glory. Eph 2:10
8

Dreams, Destiny and Goals

  • Have a clear Goal of your life. What do you want? Phil 4:13
  • Success is a result of having goals (Focus and consistency).
  • Avoid things that sabotage your goals: Laziness, bad friends, toxic social media.
